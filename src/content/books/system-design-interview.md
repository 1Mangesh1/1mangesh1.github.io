## Systems Covered

- Web Crawler

- Search Autocomplete

## What I Liked

- Clear framework for approaching any design problem
- Back-of-envelope calculations explained well
- Visual diagrams for each system

## What Could Be Better

- Some designs feel surface-level
- Would benefit from more trade-off discussions

Good starting point, but supplement with real engineering blogs for depth.
